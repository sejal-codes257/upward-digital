# Video Assets

Place your cinematic background video here:

**File name required:** `hero-video.mp4`
**Full path:** `/public/assets/videos/hero-video.mp4`

## Recommended specs:
- Format: MP4 (H.264)
- Resolution: 1920x1080 or 3840x2160 (4K)
- Duration: 15–60 seconds (loops seamlessly)
- File size: Under 15MB for fast loading
- Content: Dark, atmospheric, cinematic footage (architecture, water, light, luxury spaces)

The video is lazy-loaded (only plays when scrolled into view) and plays silently on loop.
Without the video file, the section falls back to a beautiful dark atmospheric gradient.
